York County Food Bank

Category: Food

Address: 254 W. Princess St., York, PA 17401

Phone: (717) 846-6435

The York County Food Bank serves as the central hub for fighting hunger across the entire county. It acts as a food hub that distributes millions of pounds of food to over 80 partner agencies, including local pantries and soup kitchens.

Key programs are:

  • Drive-Thru Distributions: Provides large scale food boxes for families (often enough to feed a family of four for a week).
  • Mobile Food Pantry: Brings fresh produce and staples to underserved neighborhoods.
  • Senior Box Program: Monthly supplemental food boxes specifically for low-income seniors.
  • Online Food Pantry: A modern initiative allowing residents to order groceries online for convenient pickup.

WellSpan Health

Category: Health

Address: 1001 S. George St., York, PA 17403

Phone: (717) 851-2345 / (717) 851-5320 (Crisis)

WellSpan Health is the primary integrated healthcare provider for the region. While it is a massive hospital system, its community-facing resources are what make it a top-tier resource for those in need.

Key programs are:

  • Community Health Center: It provides comprehensive primary care, pediatrics, and women's health services regardless of insurance status.
  • Crisis Intervention: A 24/7 service for mental health and substance use emergencies, providing immediate assessment and stabilization.
  • Specialized Care: Offers everything from behavioral health and oncology to tobacco education and financial counseling for medical bills.

Crispus Attucks York

Category: Employment

Address: 605 S. Duke St., York, PA 17401

Phone: (717) 848-3610

It is a historic, community-founded organization that provides a great approach to support. Named after the African American patriot, it focuses on helping individuals thrive through a wide range of life-changing services.

Key programs are:

  • Center for Employment and Training: Provides job placement, vocational training, and life skills for adults.
  • Education Services: Includes early childhood education, youth programs, and a charter school.
  • Community Development: Actively builds and manages affordable housing units in the city.
  • Active Living Center: Dedicated programming and support for seniors.
